KAORO

Our Story

Kaoro Coffee is a Costa Rican company that specializes in the sale of eco-friendly coffee. They offer a variety of products, all focused on sustainability and the quality of Costa Rican coffee. On their website, you can find organic coffee options in different presentations, including bags of ground coffee and whole beans.

how we work our products?

Eco-friendly Packaging

Our entire KAORO line is packed in compostable, airtight bags with a resealable zip-lock closure that can be easily disposed of after use without harming our beautiful Costa Rica.

Costa Rican Coffee

High-quality Costa Rican coffee with excellent flavor. Grown in volcanic soils and high-altitude tropical climate with medium body, bright acidity, and a clean finish. Flavor notes of citrus, chocolate, and nuts.

Quality Control

With roast curves created by experts for our gold coffee beans, we limit our inventory to small weekly batches and protect the longevity of the coffee in airtight bags to ensure roast freshness.
